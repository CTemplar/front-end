Why I started CTemplar

Why I started CTemplar

The idea to create this service first began when I heard about the atrocities happening in the Middle East, to the innocents and to humanity. Looking around, it would seem the larger world was too busy to pay much attention, distracted by their own affairs or helpless to offer assistance.
As just one man with limited capabilities, what could I do? Months went by as I pondered this question, finally realizing I must use my own strengths to wage a larger fight against the oppression I see in the world. As a result, I turned to technology—something I know and understand—to create a system of communication so strong and resilient I would trust it with my own life.

Through this effort to protect communications through encryption, an entirely new service has emerged, one that will never sell out to corporate investors or be weakened by international agendas— CTemplar.

Why CTemplar?

Why CTemplar?

Nearly a thousand years ago there was a group of men known as the Knights Templar. They protected the wandering pilgrims who wanted nothing more than to visit the Holy Land and find a way to achieve spirituality.

The Templar safeguarded travelers’ paths by monitoring the roadways and helping them find a successful journey. The Templar stood for righteousness, willing to lay down their lives for the poor and the weak, even if it led to their death.
Today, there are many people in the world still suffering from this same fear once felt by the innocents, as they look for ways to live their lives in peace and without intrusion. Perhaps you are one of them. I believe we now find ourselves in need of a new Templar, one that can deliver protection against the ongoing invasion of our personal lives.
Little by little, your privacy is being stolen away—as is mine—and the time is now to reclaim our rights, our freedoms, our anonymity, and to remember the strength of those who came before us, ready and willing to wield a weapon in their own defense. And for us, for modern society, that tool is technology.

Regardless of who you are and what you seek, this encrypted service is here to help you find your way. If you need assurance that your message , your vision, and your words will be protected from the eyes of those who would hamper them—this service is for you.

CTemplar’s Legal Stance

CTemplar’s Legal Stance

CTemplar’s legal team views internet privacy as a dynamic issue. It is an evolving landscape. Accordingly, static solutions will not afford your data the level of protection demanded by those who value security.

Our legal department continues to research internet security and privacy laws throughout the world so that we may constantly provide users with the maximum level of internet privacy.

We have researched all aspects of internet privacy in various jurisdictions in order to assure that our servers are domiciled in the most secure location av Our servers are located in Iceland, recognized as the premier location for internet privacy. The Icelandic government has demonstrated itself to be at the forefront of internet privacy and vigorously enforces the right to be free of government intrusion.
